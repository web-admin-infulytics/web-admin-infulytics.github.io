A platform built to empower patients and providers.

InfuLytics leverages our cloud-based data architecture to obtain, store, clean and normalize the infusion system data needed to define, create, measure and analyze clinical and operational key performance indicators, which are then integrated into reports and displayed to end-users on a dashboard in an actionable format.

Patient Reported Outcome (PRO) Applications

The integrated PRO platform allows you to create and assign customized questionnaire templates to specific patients or patient populations and send those questionnaires via text message or secure email automatically, over a programmed period of time.

The same platform also gives you the power to perform advanced analytics on incoming patient specific information, delivering actionable data that will enable clinical and operational decisions about specific patients, patient populations and clinical practice.

The PRO application allows you to effectively collect and efficiently deliver accurate, reliable and ultimately actionable clinical and operational data that will enable the success of your clinical and operational objectives.

Designed to be seamlessly integrated with existing EHR platforms

The PRO Application can be integrated with the existing EHR to save time and improve the accuracy of patient data entry and can be housed locally or accessed remotely through our HIPAA compliant, secure cloud.
